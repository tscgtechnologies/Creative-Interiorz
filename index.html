Transformation Journey

From Empty Rooms to Elegant Spaces

We believe that interior design is about more than just aesthetics—it's about creating spatial harmony that enhances your daily routine. We take raw, open structures and convert them into beautifully curated luxury dwellings.

Our integrated workflow ensures that every element of your home operates in visual and functional alignment:

  • Space Planning: Optimizing flow, circulation, and ergonomic positioning.
  • Furniture Selection: Sourcing premium, high-durability fabrics and pieces.
  • Colors & Textures: Tailoring walls, marble surfaces, and wallpaper themes.
  • Lighting Design: Seamlessly blending ambient, task, and accent lighting fixtures.
  • Art & Decor Placement: Curating customized canvas art and luxury frames.
  • Cabinetry & Hardware: Crafting flush cupboards, closets, and gold-finish hinges.
  • Appliance Planning: Embedding smart cooking systems and kitchen setups.

Our Workflow

Our Design Process

01

Consultation

First physical or virtual meeting to introduce our work, understand your layout scope, budget boundaries, and vision.

02

Requirement Discussion

Detailed session mapping the utility requirements of each family member—from study tables to bathroom lighting profiles.

03

Space Measurement

Our surveyors visit Shop no G2, Grace Residency or your site location to take down millimeter-precise wall-to-wall metrics.

04

Design Concept

Drafting initial layouts, moodboards, color schemes, and volumetric spatial distributions for bedroom and kitchen setups.

05

Material & Appliance Selection

Choosing specific laminates, hardware hinges, kitchen appliances (hobs, chimneys), marbles, and custom wall-textures.

06

Furniture & Decor Planning

Identifying specific dining table geometry, ergonomic chairs, bed headboards, custom art frames, and lighting fixtures.

07

Final Design Approval

Presenting full 2D layouts and finished specifications detail. Final budget sign-off and material scheduling.

08

Execution Support

Complete supervision on-site ensuring contractors implement the cabinetry, hardware, bathroom, and kitchen fixtures as drawn.
